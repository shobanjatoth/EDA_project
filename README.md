## EDA and Statistical Analysis of Pricing, Discounts, and Ratings in Bewakoof Products
#1. Problem Statement
In the highly competitive e-commerce fashion industry, pricing strategies, discounts, and promotional offers play a crucial role in influencing customer purchasing behavior and perception. However, it is not always clear whether higher discounts or offers actually translate into better customer satisfaction or perceived value. Bewakoof, as an online fashion brand, offers products with varying MRPs, selling prices, discounts, and offers. This project aims to analyze these factors using data-driven techniques to understand their relationships and impact on customer ratings.

#2. Project Objective
The primary objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and statistical analysis on Bewakoof product data to derive meaningful insights related to pricing, discounts, offers, and customer ratings. The analysis aims to support data-driven decision-making for pricing optimization and promotional strategies.

#3. Dataset Description
The dataset consists of product-level information collected from Bewakoof, including the following columns:
- Title: Name of the product
- Selling Price: Final price after applying discounts
- MRP: Maximum Retail Price of the product
- Discount %: Percentage discount offered on the product
- Rating: Customer rating for the product
- Offer: Promotional offer associated with the product

#4. Methodology (Step-by-Step)
Step 1: Data Understanding and Cleaning
- Load the dataset and inspect its structure
- Handle missing values and inconsistent data
- Convert data types where required
- Remove duplicate records

Step 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyze the distribution of selling price, MRP, discount percentage, and ratings
- Identify outliers and unusual pricing patterns
- Compare selling price versus MRP to understand discount effectiveness
- Segment products into price categories (budget, mid-range, premium)

Step 3: Discount and Offer Analysis
- Analyze discount percentage distribution
- Compare ratings for products with high and low discounts
- Evaluate the impact of offers on selling price and ratings

Step 4: Statistical Analysis
- Perform descriptive statistics to summarize key variables
- Conduct correlation analysis between discount percentage and ratings
- Use t-tests to compare selling prices of products with and without offers
- Apply ANOVA to compare ratings across different price segments

Step 5: Visualization and Interpretation
- Create visualizations such as histograms, boxplots, bar charts, and scatter plots
- Interpret findings to identify meaningful trends and patterns

#5. Key Insights and Outcomes
The project provides insights into how pricing, discounts, and offers influence customer ratings and perceived value. It helps identify optimal discount ranges, effective pricing strategies, and areas where discounts may not significantly improve customer satisfaction.

#6. Tools and Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy)
- Statistical Analysis Techniques
- Optional Visualization using Power BI
